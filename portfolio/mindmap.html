Continents of the Mind

Created for Geography 575 at UW-Madison, Continents of the Mind is a scrolling experience based on the work of Professor Logan Edward of UW Whitewater. This first attempt, far from complete, takes the viewer through what Edwards believes are founding aspect and principles that make up the human brain, body, mind, heart, and soul. It draws on the work of many philosphers, attempting to, much like early attempts by explorers to chart previously unexplored lands, or continents of the mind. Checkout our GitHub Repository to see initial wireframes and our code!
